# Model-Based Specifications

> Why a single model with multiple views beats flat files at scale. When your specs need to serve developers, designers, and product teams from the same source of truth.

*This guide is coming soon. In the meantime, here's what it will cover:*

- **The flat-file ceiling**: when text-based specs start breaking down (contradictions, duplication, drift)
- **What a model gives you**: one source of truth that renders as code, visuals, and documents
- **Event Modeling**: visual swimlanes with Given/When/Then, close to a full [spec dialect](/dialects/)
- **Narrative-Driven Development**: model-based specs that serve [three audiences](/dialects/narrative-driven)
- **Context management at scale**: how models solve the problems that flat files can't
- **When you don't need a model**: plenty of projects work fine with text specs forever
