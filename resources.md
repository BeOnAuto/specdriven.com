# Resources

> The essential reading, watching, and reference list for spec-driven development.
>
> Missing something? [Submit a PR](https://github.com/BeOnAuto/specdriven.com).

## Books {#books}

### Executable Specifications and BDD {#books-bdd}

- **Test-Driven Development: By Example**, Kent Beck (2002, Addison-Wesley). The book that established tests as a design activity.
- **Specification by Example**, Gojko Adzic (2011, Manning). Synthesis of the movement from 50+ teams. 2012 Jolt Award winner.
- **Bridging the Communication Gap**, Gojko Adzic (2009, Neuri). Precursor to SbE, focused on communication problems specs solve.
- **The Cucumber Book** (2nd ed.), Matt Wynne, Aslak Hellesoy, Steve Tooke (2017, Pragmatic Programmers). Authoritative Cucumber guide.
- **The Cucumber for Java Book**, Seb Rose, Matt Wynne, Aslak Hellesoy (2015, Pragmatic Programmers).
- **BDD Books: Discovery**, Seb Rose & Gaspar Nagy (2018). Collaboration techniques for discovering the right examples. First written reference on Example Mapping.
- **BDD Books: Formulation**, Seb Rose & Gaspar Nagy (2021). Capturing examples as Gherkin scenarios. Introduces B.R.I.E.F. criteria.
- **Effective Behavior-Driven Development**, Seb Rose & Gaspar Nagy (Manning, forthcoming). Consolidated guide covering Discovery, Formulation, and Automation.
- **Behavior-Driven Development with Cucumber**, Richard Lawrence & Paul Rayner (2019, Addison-Wesley). How BDD changes team collaboration.
- **BDD in Action**, John Ferguson Smart (2014, Manning). Comprehensive BDD guide.
- **Writing Great Specifications**, Kamil Nicieja (2017, Manning). Practical guide to writing effective specs.
- **Fit for Developing Software**, Rick Mugridge & Ward Cunningham (2005, Prentice-Hall). Definitive FIT guide.

### Domain-Driven Design and Collaborative Modeling {#books-ddd}

- **Domain-Driven Design**, Eric Evans (2003, Addison-Wesley). Ubiquitous Language, Bounded Contexts.
- **Implementing Domain-Driven Design**, Vaughn Vernon (2013, Addison-Wesley). Practical companion to Evans.
- **Introducing EventStorming**, Alberto Brandolini (Leanpub, in progress). Famously unfinished but invaluable.
- **The EventStorming Handbook**, Paul Rayner (Leanpub). Quick-start guide for facilitating.
- **Collaborative Software Design**, Evelyn van Kelle, Gien Verschatse, Kenny Baas-Schwegler (2024, Manning). Comprehensive guide to collaborative modeling.
- **Domain Storytelling**, Stefan Hofer & Henning Schwentner (2021, Addison-Wesley).
- **User Story Mapping**, Jeff Patton (2014, O'Reilly).
- **Impact Mapping**, Gojko Adzic (2012).
- **Visual Collaboration Tools**, Community book (Leanpub, free). Chapters on EventStorming, User Story Mapping, Domain Storytelling, and more.

- **"Understanding Spec-Driven Development: Kiro, Spec-Kit, and Tessl"**, Martin Fowler / ThoughtWorks (2025). [martinfowler.com](https://martinfowler.com/articles/exploring-gen-ai/sdd-3-tools.html).
- **"Spec-Driven Development with AI"**, GitHub Blog (2025). [github.blog](https://github.blog/ai-and-ml/generative-ai/spec-driven-development-with-ai-get-started-with-a-new-open-source-toolkit/).

- **"Spec-Driven Development with Kiro"**, Scott Logic (2025). Critical review of spec-driven tooling friction.
